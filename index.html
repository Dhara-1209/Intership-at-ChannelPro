Chhara Radio, a groundbreaking initiative, is dedicated to the preservation of our endangered Bhantu Boli. This cultural platform, manifested in the form of an app, strives to be a beacon for the richness of our heritage.

At the heart of Chhara Radio is the commitment to provide a multifaceted experience. Through literature translations, historical insights, and engaging entertainment in our dialect, the app unfolds as a dynamic repository of Bhantu Boli culture.

Immerse yourself in the captivating world of radio plays, featuring the works of profound writers and renowned pieces. This not only cultivates cultural pride but also introduces the depth and beauty of Bhantu Boli literature to a wider audience.

Chhara Radio goes beyond entertainment; it aspires to be a holistic resource for all. Offering moral stories, educational content, and career guidance, the app caters to every age group in our community.

By embracing technology, Chhara Radio becomes more than a cultural preserver; it transforms into a medium for reconnecting generations. Through this app, we ensure the seamless transmission of our culture and language, lighting the way for a brighter future.

Chhara Radio: where tradition meets technology, and the spirit of Bhantu Boli lives on.
